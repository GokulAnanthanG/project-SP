    "Beauty of Family Heart: Dr. A. Kaliyamurthy and His Family."


    Let's Explore the Essence of Our SP

     Dr.A. Kaliyamurthy was born in Budalur, Thanjavur District, had his humble beginning from a farmer’s family. His father's name is Sri.Ayyavu and his mother’s name is Smt.Kamu Ammal.

     His wife Smt. K. Suryakala is a fortunate mother of 2 children both eminent medical practitioners as also their spouses. Their son Sri. Dr. K. Ananth M. D(PAED)., daughter Smt. Dr. Pramila Rani M.D(OG)., daughter-in-law Smt. Dr. Keerthana Priyadarshini DNB(OG)., and son-in-law Sri. Dr. Karthikeyan M.S(Ortho)., are great doctors and very popular among the masses.

    "Strength in Service: Dr. A. Kaliyamurthy's life in education and medical service."


    Let's Explore the The Passion of Service

     Dr.A.Kaliyamurthy rendering low cost and free medical services in their hometown is proof of his service mind and philanthropic nature. At the same time, naming his own hospital as "SURYA HOSPITALS" shows his Love and gratitude towards his wife, Smt. K. Suryakala.

     He thoroughly believed that the country’s future lies in the classroom, and thus he performed more than thousand speeches in Schools and Colleges of Tamil Nadu in front of lakhs of students and parents and guided the students to a better future.

    "Guardian of Justice: Dr. A. Kaliyamurthy's Brave Path in Policing."


    Let's Learn About Our Police Officer

    In the All-India Police Duty Meet (AIPDM) and shooting competitions held at Nasik (1980), Lucknow (1983), Kashmir (1986) Mr. Kaliamurthy represented Tamil Nadu and won three Gold Medals. Thus, bringing honors to the state of Tamil Nadu.

    Three-Time Gold Medalist at AIPDM

    In 1996 when he was working for the Special Intelligence Department, he arrested the terrorists who were responsible for a massive explosion attack in Pandian Express. And in 2001 he also arrested the Maharashtra underground illegal mafias who were responsible for the murder of a Chennai high court lawyer, for which he was awarded The Best Intelligence Officer’s Gold Medal from Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu.

    Best Intelligence Officer’s Gold Medal (2001)

    In 2001, Sri. A. Kaliyamurthy due to his concerted efforts arrested two culprits risking his own life and was awarded the Gallantry Medal by the then Hon'ble Chief Minister, Tamil Nadu.

    Gallantry Medal (2001)

    In 2002, at Chennai in a shootout with the terrorists he was injured in the arm and in spite of the injury managed to shoot down two terrorists for which act the Tamilnadu Govt. gave him an accelerated promotion.

    Accelerated Promotion (2002)

    As Chief of a Special team, successfully eradicated rowdyism in Delta Districts within six months, gaining immense public recognition.

    Gold Medal for Eliminating Rowdyism (2006)

    Honored by President APJ Abdul Kalam for meritorious service, especially for the overnight arrest of 100 burglars in Ramji Nagar, Trichy.

    President Award (2008)

    Recognized by Mr. P. Sathasivam, former Chief Justice Supreme Court of India, for delivering motivational speeches across various platforms to inspire the youth.

    Lifetime Achievement Award

    Achieved multiple world records for speaking at numerous stages and on various topics within short durations, showcasing his oratory skills.

    World Record in Oratory (Multiple)

    Moreover, our Tamil Nadu Educational Department has honored Dr.A.Kaliyamurthy, by placing one of his speeches in our school curriculums. Especially, his inspiring speech about the importance of education placed in 11th standard general Tamil New Syllabus.

    Speech Included in School Curriculum
